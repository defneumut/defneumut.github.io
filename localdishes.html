Çorum, famous for leblebi(roasted chickpeas), is also rich in local dishes.

The most significant ones are Mayali , (Sac Mayalisi, Tava Mayalisi) , Yanic , Cizlak , Kombe, Ogmac, Hingal, Hashaslı corek, Borhani (Hamurlu, Yumurtali, Mantarli) Helise, Cullama, Madimak, Tirit , İskilip Dolması , Keskek, Kara Cuval Helvasi, Hedik, Teltel, Has Baklava. It is possible to taste these dishes in Katiple Mansion and Veli Pasa Mansion.
